Mozilla Popcorn preview

You're checking out a preview of an app in active development. This is using version 0.5.2 of the editor tools.

Mozilla Popcorn lets you bring the web into your video. This version is designed for testing and feedback purposes, but you can still play around and make something cool. When you find bugs, or have suggestions for new features, please let us know!

Popcorn will be released in late 2012. Check out our roadmap and get involved.
